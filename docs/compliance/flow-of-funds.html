1. Intended use case

Sanova Capital operates a regulated marketplace where KYC-approved investors purchase fractional shares of tokenized real estate projects. Shares are represented as ERC-4626 vault tokens on the Base blockchain.

The platform acts as merchant of record for the investment transaction. Licensed third-party payment providers collect fiat or facilitate crypto on-ramp on our behalf. All on-chain investment settlement is denominated in USDC on Base.

Investor funds are used exclusively to:

  • Settle the investor’s purchase of RWA vault shares
  • Pay platform and advisor commissions (USDC, on-chain)
  • Fund property SPV vaults that back the tokenized assets

Funds are not used for FX speculation, crypto trading, or unrelated third-party transfers.

7. Example transaction (Mercado Pago — Argentina)

  1. Investor purchases USD 1,000 in vault shares (+ buyer fees ≈ USD 1,050 total).
  2. Investor pays the ARS equivalent via Mercado Pago wallet balance.
  3. On-ramp converts to approximately 1,050 USDC delivered to Sanova treasury on Base.
  4. Platform verifies the on-chain transaction and confirms the purchase.
  5. ~1,000 USDC allocated to the property vault; ~50 USDC commissions to advisors/platform.
  6. Investor receives ERC-4626 vault shares in their linked wallet.
